Looking for some guides on how to structure your days exploring Pittsburgh? Here are some example itineraries so that you can get the most out of all the food spots in Pittsburgh! (based on the places that I've been to so far) Below, you can find some of the food places grouped by location (Squirrel Hill, Oakland, Shadyside, Strip District, Downtown, Lawrenceville)

Squirrel Hill:

  1. Breakfast at Upstreet Diner
  2. Work at Commonplace Coffee
  3. Lunch at Thai Noodle Outlet
  4. Explore stores on Murray and Forbes!
  5. Grab boba at Tsaocaa
  6. Dinner at Cafe 33
  7. Dessert at Waffallonia

Oakland:

  1. Breakfast at Pamela's Diner
  2. Coffee and work at Redhawk Cafe
  3. Lunch at Oishii Bento
  4. Explore nearby museums
  5. Snack and work at Divvy Coffee and Buns
  6. Dinner at the Porch
  7. Dessert from Dave and Andy's

Shadyside:

  1. Breakfast at Cafe Moulin
  2. Work at Thee Coffee Tree Roasters
  3. Pizza and gelato at Mercurio's
  4. Shop around (there are some nice clothing, arts and crafts, and book stores)
  5. Dinner at Tacoya Taqueria
  6. Dessert from Treat

Strip District:

  1. Brunch at Caffe Mona
  2. Drinks at De Fer Coffee & Tea
  3. Lunch at Coop De Ville
  4. Walk around the strip and explore the all the little shops
  5. Snack from Peace, Love, and Little Donuts
  6. Dinner at Little Bangkok
  7. Dessert at Klavon's Ice Cream Parlor

Downtown

  1. Breakfast at The Speckled Egg
  2. Coffee from Starbucks
  3. Lunch at Sally Ann's
  4. Explore downtown!
  5. Dinner at Yuzu Kitchen
  6. Stop by Point State Park for sunset
  7. Dessert at Milkshake Factory

Lawrenceville

  1. Brunch at Gepetto Cafe
  2. Catch a movie at Row House Cinema
  3. Browse through some of the stores
  4. Dinner at Condado Tacos
  5. Walk on the bridge for sunset
  6. Dessert at NatuRoll Creamery
